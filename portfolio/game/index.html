A magical colouring experience
for young travellers

From now to 28 February 2020 . children unter the age of nine travelling on selected Singapore Airlines flights will recevice a complimentray colouring boook featuring four friends from Rainforest Lumina at the Singapore Zoo.

Use it with the Colour Me Magic! companion mobile app to see your child's colouring come to life --exactly as they've coloured them!

To enjoy the magical colouring experience on board, download the free Colour Me Magical! companion mobile app before your flight.

How it Works

Colour

Receive your Colour Me Magic! colouring book on board the flight and start colouring.

SCAN

Use the Colour Me Magic! mobile app to scan the page and watch as your animal friends come to life!

PLAY

Play a game with your animal friends or take a selfie with them to earn badges. Collect all four badges to win prizes*!
