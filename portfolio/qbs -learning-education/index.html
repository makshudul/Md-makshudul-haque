Welcome to QSB

Meet QBS Learning

We Transform Lives.

At QBS Learning, we believe in the power of education to transform lives. We’re a forward-thinking educational company with editorial expertise, and design savvy for both textbook and digital product development. We’re committed to enriching the lives of millions of Pre-K through Grade 12 students to help them achieve their dreams.

We Collaborate.

QBS is a global content partner, collaborating with publishers based around the world to create the finest educational materials in all curriculum areas. That’s why we’ve set up our headquarters in New York, with a delivery center in New Delhi, India, and satellite offices in London and Dubai. Our workforce of 500 people develop materials for both the home and school market that meet the ever-evolving demands placed on parents and educators around the world.

We Create to Inspire.

We think "out-of-the-box!" For more than 30 years, our company has developed innovative digital and print materials that inspire students of all ages, while meeting the most demanding U. S. curriculum standards as well as standards from countries across the globe.

We Get the Job Done.

Whether creating an inventive prototype or problem-solving how to meet an aggressive schedule, our company will rise to the occasion to meet our clients’ needs with creativity, thoroughness, and a can-do spirit.
