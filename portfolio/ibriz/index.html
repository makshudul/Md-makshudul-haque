Who Are We?

An innovative cleantech company building a brighter future

By designing, manufacturing, financing and distributing solar solutions for rural communities, we’re committed to making a real difference.

Not only that, but we partner with women’s charities, agricultural cooperatives, NGO and MFI to empower rural economies from the ground up.

Our Mission

To provide affordable clean energy for all

By working with off-grid communities to build a brighter future.

We empower rural female entrepreneurs and the next generation through the agribusiness value chain.

We Offer

End to end solar solutions for everyone

From market-driven solutions to accessible financing, everything we offer is designed to make the world a better place.

Each device is securely monitored, pay as you go enabled, and supported by our intuitive digital platform.

We provide productive use systems

Such as turnkey agro-processing stations, smart solar irrigation, and solar cooling systems. Our approach supports local farmers, women-led businesses, and the next generation.

We provide financing

In partnership with local institutions, and intelligently power our solutions with IoT systems and a digital payment platform.

Our prices are simple, fair and affordable for all.

We provide training and after sale services

We want our customers to learn from us, not just buy from us. That’s why we’re committed to passing on our knowledge so every community can enjoy a brighter future.
