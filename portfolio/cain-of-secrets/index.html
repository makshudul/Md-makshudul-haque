Values

These are Chain of Secrets value’s that we model each project we embark on within the enigma community.

Reliability

All our infrastructure is housed in secure datacenter’s around the world. We are constantly building redundnacy into our infrastructure to ensure perpetual node coverage and network uptime.

Engagement

Our mission is to build engagement on the Engima network with exciting new tools, applications & services to use.

Trust

We believe in building decentralization and will provide our projects to the Enigma community in an opensource manner.
