Prees Bowling Club

  • HOME
  • SPORTS & ACTIVITIES
  • BOWLING

The club has been running since 1933 and has been a great location for bowling ever since. The idea of a bowling club at Prees was first mooted in 1932 when it was thought that a bowling club might be a social asset to the community.

The club started with one green which was laid in 1933 and is still in use today having been reseeded in 2005 and extended over the years. A larger green, No. 2 green, was opened in 1977, which was subsequently replaced with a newer No. 2 green. The original No. 2 green was sold for housing development to raise funds for the new club house which was opened in December 2005.

The club currently has over 100 registered bowling members playing in four leagues and club competitions during the summer.
