Protect Your Account

Use strong passwords

Ensure you are using a strong and UNIQUE password on your account. Never reuse passwords across multiple accounts.

Enable 2FA on your account

Enroll a Login 2FA method to keep your account safe. Authenticator apps are good, but security tokens are BEST.

Enable Settings Lock

Enable Settings Lock to prevent unauthorized changes to your account and hide sensitive information.

Keep your funds safe

Keep the majority of your crypto offline in a hardware wallet. Only keep the funds you need for trading and other activities on Kraken.
